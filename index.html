Ontario Digital Education Platform

Ministry of Education | Toronto, ON

Sept 2024 - Apr 2025

This artifact is the Ontario Digital Education Platform where teachers and students can access their curriculums digitally. It was launched in June 2020, accessed by over 1 million users annually, and aims to be user-centric and inclusive.

During my time with the ministry, I performed manual functionality, performance and security testing across multiple environments for two separate releases (Release 13 and Release 14), while adhering to Agile methodology.

For Release 13, my main focus was AODA WCAG 2.1 compliance. In addition, I performed rigorous testing on the search pages, resources pages, and another note-taking feature, which was later discontinued indefinitely.

Release 14 introduces a new search feature showcased on the homepage, designed to help users easily navigate and find specific subjects or curriculums! (The feature title remains undisclosed as Release 14 has yet to be launched.) Main Tools used - Azure, Excel, Kontent
